Our Firm – About Defence & Co.

Founded in 2005, Defence & Co. has represented over 1,200 clients in complex criminal cases across New South Wales. Our solicitors combine decades of courtroom experience with a dedication to client care.

We handle matters from minor offences to serious indictable charges, always ensuring our clients understand every step of the process.
